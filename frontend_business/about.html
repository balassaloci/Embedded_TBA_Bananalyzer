Motivation

According to Banana Link, a UK not-for-profit organisation, over 5 billion bananas are eaten every year in Britain. On average, each person in the UK eats 10kg of bananas every year, which is equivalent to 100 bananas. However, 1 in every 10 bananas is thrown away uneaten, meaning that every person wastes 1kg of bananas each year on average. This accumulates to 500 million wasted bananas yearly in Britain.

Target market

The primary target market for Bananalyzer are people who eat bananas regularly. The secondary market includes people who don't necessarily eat bananas, but have pets that eat a lot of bananas (primarily monkeys).

Market survey

The goal of our market research was to help us estimate the need for our product and to learn about current customer needs.

In this survey, a total of 63 people were provided a description of Bananalyzer and asked for their feedback. The study was carried out through social media, reaching a wide range of potential customers. The table below summarizes their response.

It is clear from the market survey that there is a market need for such a product.
