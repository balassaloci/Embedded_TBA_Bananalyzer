Introduction

Are you a banana enthusiast? Or a monkey owner?

Your bananas often get rotten?

By using Bananalyzer, you will never ever have to worry about this problem again.

Bananalyzer is a small and simple product, that you can put next to your bananas in your home and it will continuously monitor the status of your bananas.

If you buy green bananas, the device tells you when they become ripe. If your bananas are already ripe, it reminds you to eat them before they got rotten.

How does it work?

The "soul" of Bananalyzer is a colour and temperature sensor connected to a microcontroller, gathering information about the bananas. The colour sensor gives us data about the current state of the banana (unripe/ripe/rotten), while the temperature sensor helps in estimating when the banana gets ripe/rotten.

The device has a wifi module, so it uploads the data to our server, where statistical data is calculated and displayed on a website, that you can access privately. You can access this website from any device with internet connectivity (phones, tablets, computers, etc.) both from home and outside of it. The website shows you historical data about your previously bought bananas and current data about the bananas you have at the moment. Several graphs are available, such as average lifetime, number of bananas wasted, and the predicted time your current bananas will remain eatable.

All you have to do is place the device next to your bananas, connect it to your wifi and enjoy your delicious, ripe bananas.
